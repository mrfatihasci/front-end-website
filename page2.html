How and Why

HOW & WHY Learning from a correct solution make you understand how, but not why. When you try to build a unique-creative page, you dont usually do it in the best way, because you dont know WHY. To learn how & why in html & css, we better teach you what to do as well as what not do, this saves time on the long term. instead of seeing how others do it the correct way.
